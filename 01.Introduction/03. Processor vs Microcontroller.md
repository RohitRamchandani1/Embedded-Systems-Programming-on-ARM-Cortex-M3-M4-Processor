# Cortex M4 Processor vs Microcontroller
- The Microcontroller i.e. STM32F44RE uses the ARM Cortex M4.
- All the peripherals to the Processor(Cortex M4) are developed by the STMicroelectronics.
- Some of the MSP CPUs are developed by TI itself not by ARM.
- ARM Cortex M4 acts as a master and all the peripherals act as slave.
